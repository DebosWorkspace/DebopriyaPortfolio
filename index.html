About Me

"Hi, I’m Debopriya — a passionate learner and creative thinker. My journey began in school, where I was always curious about how things worked, especially in the world of technology. Over time, this curiosity grew into a strong interest in coding and web development. Currently, I’m pursuing a B.Tech in Information Technology at Narula Institute of Technology. Alongside my studies, I’m actively learning front-end development, exploring HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while gradually moving into full-stack development. I enjoy building clean, responsive websites and am always eager to learn new tools and frameworks. Apart from tech, photography has been a creative outlet for me. I love capturing nature, atmospheric scenes, and everyday beauty through my lens. It helps me see the world differently and adds balance to my technical side. I also have a deep interest in languages—both programming and human languages—and I believe in constantly improving myself, whether it’s through learning a new skill or becoming more disciplined and self-aware. My goal is to combine creativity with technology and keep growing as a developer and a person."
